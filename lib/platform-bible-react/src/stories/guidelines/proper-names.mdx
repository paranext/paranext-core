## Products

### Paratext 10 Studio

- **Short name:** Studio (especially when compared to Platform or Paratext 9)
- Paratext 10 Studio is the next generation of Paratext, built on Platform.Bible. "Studio" refers to all versions after Paratext 9.
- **Website:** [studio.paratext.org](https://studio.paratext.org) (also [paratext.bible](https://paratext.bible))

### Paratext 9

- **Short name:** Paratext 9 (sometimes just "Paratext" in legacy contexts)
- The previous major version, widely used for Bible translation projects.
- **Website:** [www.paratext.org](https://www.paratext.org) (future: [9.paratext.org](https://9.paratext.org))

### Platform.Bible

- **Short name:** Platform (especially when compared to Studio or Paratext 9)
- The open-source, extensible editor underlying Paratext 10 Studio. Designed for Bible translation teams.
- **Website:** [platform.bible](https://platform.bible) (redirects to [platform.paratext.org](https://platform.paratext.org))

## Renamed Features

### Sync (formerly Send/Receive)

"Send/Receive" is now called **Sync** — a more familiar term that reflects a simpler, more reliable implementation requiring less user intervention.
